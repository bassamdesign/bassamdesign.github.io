About the game
Dive into a world of vibrant lanes, bustling intersections, and engaging car puzzles.
This is not an ordinary matching puzzle game. It's a unique blend of match 3 excitement and the thrill of traffic jams. With eye-catching graphics and intuitive gameplay, each level presents a fresh challenge to clear car traffic by matching the suitable vehicles in a row or column.
