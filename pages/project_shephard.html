Level Designer


Setting
Along with the other designer we came up with the idea to interject Shephard from the previous Half-Life game in another part of City 17 as the main story of Half-Life:Alyx is taking place.

Layouts & Locations
Prototyped & tested an underground prison/lab where the main character escapes at the start that leads into a train station. This first level was scraped due to time restirction & focused on the train station.
Set Dressing


Object Placement
Cluttered the world with objects that belong to each room of the building & in-line with VR level design priniciples that ground the player without overwhelming them.

Texturing
Textured everything in the level alongside decals to show decay.
